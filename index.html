Automatic LPG Cylinder Booking And Leakage Detection:
-- Gas leakage is a major problem in residential premises as well as in industries that work on LPG. This project is built to prevent gas leakage and to ensure household safety where appliances use liquid petroleum gas may be a source of risk. A gas sensor MQ6 is used which detects leakage of gas and indicates the consumer by activating the buzzers as well as by sending messages through the GSM module and simultaneously activates the exhaust fan and closes the solenoid valve (cylinder knob). The additional advantage of this project is that it continuously monitors the weight of the cylinder through the load cell which is directly connected to Arduino. If it exceeds the specified weight, them it automatically books the cylinder via GSM module.
